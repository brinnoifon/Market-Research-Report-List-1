<p><ul><li>X Band Radar</li><li>S Band Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine Radar market is primarily divided into two types: X Band Radar and S Band Radar. X Band Radar operates on shorter wavelengths, providing high-resolution images, making it ideal for detecting small objects and navigating in congested waters. In contrast, S Band Radar utilizes longer wavelengths that penetrate precipitation better, offering improved range and performance in adverse weather. Both types serve vital functions in marine navigation, collision avoidance, and maritime safety, catering to diverse operational needs across the shipping industry.</p></p>
