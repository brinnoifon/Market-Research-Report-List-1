<p><p>The Automotive eCall market is categorized into two main types: automatic and manual button systems. Automatic eCall systems activate upon a vehicle collision, using built-in sensors to detect the impact and automatically transmit emergency alerts along with critical crash data to emergency services. In contrast, manual button systems allow the vehicle occupants to initiate the emergency call by pressing a dedicated button in the vehicle, providing assistance in situations where an automatic trigger may not occur. Both types enhance road safety and emergency response efficiency.</p></p>
