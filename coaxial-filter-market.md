<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Coaxial Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coaxial filter market is divided into two primary types: passive and active filters. Passive coaxial filters consist of inductors, capacitors, and resistors, utilizing these components to process signals without external power. They are known for simplicity and reliability. In contrast, active coaxial filters incorporate amplifying devices like transistors, requiring external power to enhance signal processing capabilities. Active filters offer better performance in terms of selectivity and gain, making them suitable for more complex applications in communication systems.</p></p>
