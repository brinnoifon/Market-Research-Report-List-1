<p><p>The market for transparent ceramic substrates features various types, each distinguished by their material properties and applications. Yttria transparent ceramics provide excellent thermal stability, suitable for high-temperature applications. Spinel transparent ceramics are lightweight and strong, often utilized in military optics. YAG transparent ceramics are favored for laser applications due to their efficient light transmission. Other types may include emerging materials with specific functionalities.</p></p>
