<p><p>The solenoid dosing pump market can be categorized into two main types: manual control and signal control. Manual control pumps require direct operator input to adjust flow rates and dosing parameters, offering simplicity and flexibility for various applications. In contrast, signal control pumps utilize automated signals, often from external controllers or sensors, to regulate dosing accurately based on real-time data. This automated approach enhances precision and efficiency, making it suitable for complex processes in industries like water treatment and chemical dosing.</p></p>
