<p><p>The DNA polymerase market is categorized into two primary types: prokaryotic and eukaryotic DNA polymerases. In contrast, eukaryotic DNA polymerases are obtained from eukaryotic organisms and play vital roles in DNA synthesis, repair, and replication in eukaryotic cells. Both types have distinct functions and applications in research, diagnostics, and therapeutic areas, driving market growth.</p></p>
