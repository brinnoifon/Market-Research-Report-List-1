<p><p>The medical isolator market comprises two main types: floor-standing and mobile types. Floor-standing isolators are stationary units designed for permanent placement in laboratories or hospitals, providing controlled environments for sensitive procedures. They ensure high containment and sterility, often equipped with advanced filtration systems. In contrast, mobile isolators are portable, allowing for flexibility in usage across different locations within medical facilities. They facilitate quick access to sterile conditions wherever needed, supporting various medical and research applications.</p></p>
