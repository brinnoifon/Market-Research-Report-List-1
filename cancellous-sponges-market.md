<p><p>Cancellous sponges, utilized in various medical applications, are available in two primary forms: strips and blocks. Strips are thin, elongated pieces, ideal for filling irregular bone defects and ensuring stability in surgical sites. In contrast, blocks are larger, cubic pieces suited for extensive reconstruction procedures, providing a solid support scaffold for bone growth. Both types promote natural bone regeneration and are essential in orthopedics and dental surgeries, catering to different surgical needs and enhancing patient recovery.</p></p>
