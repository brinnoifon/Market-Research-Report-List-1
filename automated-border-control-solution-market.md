<p><p>The Automated Border Control (ABC) solution market comprises primarily two types: ABC E-gates and ABC Kiosks. ABC E-gates are integrated entry points that use biometric recognition to facilitate quick and secure passage for travelers, enhancing flow at borders. In contrast, ABC Kiosks are self-service stations where travelers can independently verify their identity, often incorporating document scanning and biometric checks. Both solutions aim to streamline border control processes, improve security, and reduce wait times for travelers.</p></p>
