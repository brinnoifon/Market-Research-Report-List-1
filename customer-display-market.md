<p><p>The customer display market primarily includes LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) and VFD (Vacuum Fluorescent Display) technologies. LCDs are favored for their high-resolution visuals and energy efficiency, making them suitable for modern retail environments. They support a wide range of colors and graphics. In contrast, VFDs are known for their bright, clear text and durability, often used in point-of-sale systems. While LCDs offer versatile display capabilities, VFDs provide a retro aesthetic and superior visibility in low-light conditions, catering to different customer preferences.</p></p>
