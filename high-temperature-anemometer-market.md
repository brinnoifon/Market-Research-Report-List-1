<p><p>The high-temperature anemometer market includes various types designed to measure air velocity in extreme conditions. Vane anemometers utilize rotating blades to determine wind speed, making them suitable for various applications. Cup anemometers use three or four cups attached to a shaft that spins in the wind, providing accurate measurements of airflow. Both types are essential in fields like industrial processes, meteorology, and HVAC, where monitoring high-temperature environments is crucial for efficiency and safety.</p></p>
