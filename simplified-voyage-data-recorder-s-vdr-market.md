<p><strong>The Simplified Voyage Data Recorder (S-VDR) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float Free Capsule S-VDR</li><li>Fixed Capsule S-VDR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Simplified Voyage Data Recorder (S-VDR) market comprises two main types: Float Free Capsule S-VDR and Fixed Capsule S-VDR. Float Free Capsule S-VDRs are designed to automatically detach and float to the surface in emergencies, enhancing recovery chances. In contrast, Fixed Capsule S-VDRs are securely mounted on the ship, providing continuous data recording but requiring retrieval under specific conditions. Both types play a vital role in maritime safety and compliance by capturing critical voyage data for post-incident analysis.</p></p>
