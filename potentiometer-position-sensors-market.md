<p><strong>The Potentiometer Position Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Potentiometer Rotary Position Sensors</li><li>Potentiometer Linear Position Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The potentiometer position sensors market encompasses two primary types: rotary and linear position sensors. Potentiometer rotary position sensors measure angular displacement, often used in applications like joysticks and knobs, providing precise rotational feedback. Meanwhile, potentiometer linear position sensors detect linear movement along a straight path, commonly found in applications such as displacement measurement in machinery and automotive settings. Both sensor types rely on the variable resistance principle, ensuring accurate position sensing in various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
