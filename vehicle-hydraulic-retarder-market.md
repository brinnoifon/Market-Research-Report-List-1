<p><p>The vehicle hydraulic retarder market comprises two main types: water medium retarders and oil medium retarders. Water medium retarders utilize water as a cooling fluid, providing effective heat dissipation and better performance in terms of energy efficiency, making them suitable for high-traffic applications. In contrast, oil medium retarders use synthetic or mineral oils, offering superior lubrication and protection against wear under extreme conditions, often preferred for heavy-duty vehicles. Both types enhance braking performance and vehicle safety, reducing wear on traditional brakes.</p></p>
