<p><p>The canal hearing aids market is primarily divided into three types: Completely in Canal (CIC), In the Canal (ITC), and Invisible in Canal (IIC). CIC aids fit snugly within the ear canal, offering a discreet option with moderate amplification. ITC models are slightly larger and fill a portion of the ear canal, providing more features and better battery life. IIC aids are designed to be virtually invisible, sitting deep in the canal for a natural sound experience, ideal for those seeking aesthetics.</p></p>
