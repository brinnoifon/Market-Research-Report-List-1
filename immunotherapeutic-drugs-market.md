<p><p>The immunotherapeutic drugs market comprises various types, including monoclonal antibodies, vaccines, checkpoint inhibitors, and interferons. Monoclonal antibodies target specific antigens to enhance immune response against diseases. Vaccines stimulate the immune system to recognize and combat pathogens or tumors. Checkpoint inhibitors block proteins that inhibit immune responses, allowing for stronger anti-tumor activity. Interferons are signaling proteins that modulate the immune response and have antiviral and anti-proliferative effects, contributing to the treatment of certain cancers and viral infections.</p></p>
