<p><p>The refrigerant market comprises various types, including HCFCs (Hydrochlorofluorocarbons), HFCs (Hydrofluorocarbons), and HCs (Hydrocarbons). HCFCs are being phased out due to their ozone depletion potential but are still used in some applications. HFCs are more environmentally friendly but contribute to global warming and are also facing reduction initiatives. HCs, such as propane and butane, are natural refrigerants with low environmental impact. Other refrigerants include CO2 and ammonia, offering alternatives with different efficiency and safety profiles.</p></p>
