<p><p>Anaerobic workstation markets are categorized by the number of gases they manage. Single gas systems typically control an environment with only one gas, often nitrogen, to create anaerobic conditions. Dual gas systems use a combination, like nitrogen and carbon dioxide, to optimize growth conditions for specific microorganisms. Three gas workstations offer a more complex atmosphere by incorporating three gases, allowing for precise control over the environment, which is essential for advanced microbiological and cell culture applications.</p></p>
