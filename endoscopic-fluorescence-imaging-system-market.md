<p><p>The Endoscopic Fluorescence Imaging System market is primarily divided into two types: Visible Fluorescence Imaging Systems and Near-Infrared Fluorescence Imaging Systems. Visible Fluorescence Imaging Systems utilize visible light to detect fluorescence signals from specific markers within tissues, aiding in diagnostics and surgical guidance. In contrast, Near-Infrared Fluorescence Imaging Systems operate at longer wavelengths, allowing deeper tissue penetration and enhanced imaging of vascular structures and tumors, thus providing improved contrast and resolution in minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
