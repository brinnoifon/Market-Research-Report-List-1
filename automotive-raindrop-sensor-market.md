<p><p>The automotive raindrop sensor market is primarily divided into two types: analog sensors and digital sensors. Analog sensors provide continuous output signals that vary in response to changing rain conditions, offering a straightforward measurement of rainfall intensity. In contrast, digital sensors convert raindrop detection into binary signals, enabling more precise and robust data processing. Both types enhance vehicle functionalities, such as automatic windshield wipers, improving driver safety and comfort by ensuring optimal visibility during adverse weather conditions.</p></p>
