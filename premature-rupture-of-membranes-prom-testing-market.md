<p><p>The Premature Rupture of Membranes (PROM) testing market includes several diagnostic types. The Nitrazine/pH test detects amniotic fluid by measuring vaginal pH levels. The Ferning test identifies fluid's characteristic crystallization pattern under a microscope. The Amnisure test employs immunoassay technology for specific detection of amniotic fluid proteins. Other testing methods may include ultrasound assessments or visual examinations, contributing to the overall understanding and management of PROM in pregnant women, thereby guiding clinical decision-making and treatment options.</p></p>
