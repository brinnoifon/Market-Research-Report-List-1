<p><p>Pharmacy management software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and easier updates, as users can access it from anywhere with an internet connection. In contrast, on-premise solutions are installed directly on local servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance. Both types aim to enhance operational efficiency, inventory management, and patient care in pharmacies.</p></p>
