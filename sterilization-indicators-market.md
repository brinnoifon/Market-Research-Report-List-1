Ethylene oxide sterilization indicators confirm that this gas has penetrated materials effectively. Hydrogen peroxide sterilization indicators ensure that the vapor-phase sterilization process was successful. Steam sterilization indicators signal whether steam has reached the necessary parameters to achieve sterilization. Each type serves specific applications within sterilization protocols in medical and laboratory settings.</p></p>
