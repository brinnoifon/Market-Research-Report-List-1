<p><p>The Flexible Extraction Arm market comprises three main types: wall-mounted, ceiling-mounted, and bench-top systems. Wall-mounted arms are designed to optimize space by being affixed to vertical surfaces, facilitating ease of use without occupying floor space. Ceiling-mounted units utilize overhead spaces, providing flexibility in positioning and extensive reach. Bench-top extraction arms are compact and designed for direct use on work surfaces, ideal for small workshops or labs. Each type addresses specific operational needs while enhancing safety and air quality.</p></p>
