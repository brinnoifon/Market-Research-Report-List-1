<p><p>The Gas-insulated Transformers (GIT) market comprises two main types: full-insulated transformers and semi-insulated transformers. Full-insulated transformers utilize a completely sealed design, offering enhanced safety and reliability, making them suitable for high-voltage applications. In contrast, semi-insulated transformers feature a combination of gas insulation and air insulation, balancing cost and performance while still providing substantial protection against environmental factors. Both types are gaining traction in various industries, particularly in urban areas where space and safety are paramount.</p></p>
