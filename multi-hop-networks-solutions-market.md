<p><p>Multi-hop networks solutions encompass Mobile Ad Hoc Networks (MANETs) and Multi-hop Cellular Networks. MANETs are decentralized, enabling devices to connect directly and share data without fixed infrastructure, making them ideal for dynamic environments. In contrast, Multi-hop Cellular Networks extend cellular coverage by allowing devices to relay signals to each other, improving connectivity in remote areas. Both types leverage multiple nodes to enhance communication efficiency and resilience, catering to diverse applications from emergency response to rural connectivity and disaster recovery.</p></p>
