<p><p>The automotive radar sensors market encompasses various technologies, including CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) types. CMOS sensors offer benefits such as lower power consumption, high-speed processing, and integration capabilities, making them increasingly popular in automotive applications like adaptive cruise control and collision avoidance. CCD sensors, known for their superior image quality and sensitivity, are still used but are less common in modern automotive systems due to higher energy requirements and cost. Both technologies contribute to enhancing vehicle safety and automation.</p></p>
