<p><ul><li>On Premises Server</li><li>Cloud Server</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Home media servers can be categorized into two main types: on-premises servers and cloud servers. On-premises servers are physical devices located at home, allowing users to store and manage their media libraries locally, providing faster access and greater control over content. Cloud servers, on the other hand, leverage off-site storage solutions, enabling users to stream and access their media from anywhere with an internet connection, offering convenience and scalability, but relying on internet connectivity for performance.</p></p>
