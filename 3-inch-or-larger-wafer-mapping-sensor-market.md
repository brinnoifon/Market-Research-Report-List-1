<p><p>The 3-inch or larger wafer mapping sensor market includes two primary types: LED beam mapping sensors and laser beam mapping sensors. LED beam mapping sensors utilize light-emitting diodes to illuminate the wafer surface, providing detailed imaging of defects and irregularities. In contrast, laser beam mapping sensors employ focused laser beams to achieve high-precision measurements and can capture finer details, making them suitable for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. Both types play critical roles in ensuring quality and efficiency in wafer production processes.</p></p>
