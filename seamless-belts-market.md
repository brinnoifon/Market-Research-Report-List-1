<p><p>The seamless belts market is categorized into two main types: synchronous endless substrates and non-synchronous endless substrates. Synchronous endless substrates are designed for precise timing and coordination in machinery, ensuring synchronized motion for applications like conveyors and automation. In contrast, non-synchronous endless substrates are versatile and used in applications requiring flexible speed and load handling. Both types serve industries such as manufacturing, packaging, and logistics, catering to specific operational needs and enhancing efficiency in material transport and processing.</p></p>
