<p><p>The multipurpose drainage catheter market is categorized primarily into four types based on material: rubber, plastic (PVC), silicone, and other materials. Rubber catheters offer flexibility and durability but may be less biocompatible. Plastic (PVC) catheters are lightweight and cost-effective but can be less flexible. Silicone catheters provide excellent biocompatibility and resistance to kinking, making them preferred for long-term use. Other materials may include specialty polymers or composite materials designed for specific applications, enhancing performance and safety in various medical scenarios.</p></p>
