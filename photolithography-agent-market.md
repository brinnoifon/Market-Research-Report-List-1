<p><strong>The Photolithography Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ArF Immersion</li><li>ArF Dry</li><li>KrF</li><li>I-Line</li><li>G-Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photolithography agent market is categorized into several types based on the light wavelength used. ArF (Argon Fluoride) immersion uses shorter wavelengths for high-resolution patterns, ideal for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. ArF dry employs similar technology but in a non-immersion mode. KrF (Krypton Fluoride) operates at slightly longer wavelengths, suitable for smaller feature sizes. I-Line and G-Line utilize even longer wavelengths, often used for less intricate layers in integrated circuits. Each type serves specific applications in the lithography process, balancing resolution and cost.</p></p>
