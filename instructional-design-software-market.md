<p><p>Instructional design software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software is hosted online, allowing users to access tools and resources from anywhere with internet connectivity. This model typically offers easier collaboration and scalability. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed directly on a user's local hardware, providing more control over data and customization options but requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance.
